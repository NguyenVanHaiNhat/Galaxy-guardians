Galaxy Guardians

Author: Nguyễn Văn Hải Nhật

Description:

Control your plane with the mouse cursor, press space to shoot (can be pressed to support the button)

If you collide with an object or a monster's bullet, everything will not end. If you have 5 lives, if you lose all 5 lives, you will lose.

Destroying enemies will give you points and for every 100 point you will get 1 extra life
